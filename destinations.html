Destination: Skardu

Welcome to Skardu! Nestled in the scenic landscapes of Pakistan, Skardu is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Things to Do

  • Visit Shangrila Resort and Lower Kachura Lake
  • Explore Skardu Fort and Sheosar Lake
  • Hike in the mesmerizing Deosai National Park

Road to Skardu

The journey to Skardu is an adventure in itself. The road takes you through breathtaking valleys and mountain passes, providing stunning views of the Karakoram Range.

Weather

Skardu experiences a cold desert climate with chilly winters and mild summers. The best time to visit is during the summer months (May to September) when the weather is pleasant for outdoor activities.

Tourist Guide

Ensure you explore the local markets for traditional crafts and try the delicious Balti cuisine. Plan your itinerary to cover the major attractions and engage in activities like river rafting in the Indus River.
